FEATURES
Offline Location Tracking
 Lured leverages cutting-edge technology to enable offline location tracking, allowing users to locate their devices even when mobile data and location services are disabled. By utilizing alternative methods such as Wi-Fi and Bluetooth signals, Lured ensures that users can always find their devices, regardless of their current settings.
Continuous Updates
Lured continuously monitors the device's location and updates the user with real-time information, ensuring that they stay informed about their phone's status at all times. Whether the device is stationary or on the move, Lured provides accurate and up-to-date location data, giving users peace of mind in any situation.
Log Reporting
In addition to real-time location updates, Lured generates detailed log reports that provide insights into the device's activity history. From location history to battery status, users can access comprehensive reports that help them understand their phone's usage patterns and track its movements over time.
Customizable Alerts
Lured offers customizable alert settings that allow users to set up notifications for specific events, such as device movement outside a designated area or low battery levels. With customizable alerts, users can proactively monitor their device's status and take action when necessary.
Secure Access
Lured prioritizes user privacy and security, ensuring that only authorized individuals have access to the app's location tracking features. With robust authentication mechanisms and encryption protocols in place, users can trust that their personal information remains protected at all times.
